About the creator

Hi! I'm Catherine! I am a 25 year old Chinese-Indonesian American woman residing in Atlanta, Georgia currently a student at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor in the process of obtaining a Masters degree in Information. My focus is in User Experience (UX) and Human Computer Interaction with the interest of Virtual Reality (VR).

I created the VegOut to help individuals who have dietary needs and restrictions to easily find restaurants near them easier. This site includes the necessities like searching through a local restaurant near you, the ability to click on images that redirect you to other websites, and learning more ways that the community can accomodate you.

Vegout, so you can eat in. It should be easy for you to eat inside a restaurant.
